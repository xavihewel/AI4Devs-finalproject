Lean Canvas — Corporate Carpool (MVP)

Structured like the traditional LC: 5 columns × 2 rows, plus bottom row (Cost Structure ×3, Revenue/Benefits ×2).

Problem
  • High commuting costs (fuel, parking, tolls)
  • Few sustainable daily options
  • Low vehicle occupancy
  • Company goal to reduce CO2 & improve wellbeing
Solution (MVP)
  • SSO login
  • Minimal profile (zone, role, schedule)
  • Publish rides (driver)
  • Search & reserve (rider)
  • Basic confirmations/notifications
Unique Value Proposition
  • Internal & secure carpooling tailored to employees
  • Reduces cost & coordination friction
  • Cuts parking pressure and CO2
  • Integrates with corporate tools (SSO, calendar, intranet)
Unfair Advantage
  • Private data & GDPR compliance
  • Fit to culture & internal policies
  • Incentives & reporting aligned with company goals
Customer Segments
  • Employees (drivers & riders)
  • HR & Sustainability (reporting)
  • Facilities/Operations (parking & mobility)
Existing Alternatives
  • Spreadsheets / email
  • Generic carpool apps
  • Shuttles / unmanaged parking
  • Third-party tools (Hoop, KINTO, etc.)
Key Metrics
  • Registered / active employees
  • Trips per week & average occupancy
  • CO2 avoided (shared km)
  • Repeat / retention rate
High-Level Concept
  • Simple corporate platform to coordinate daily carpooling
Channels
  • Intranet / newsletter / signage
  • Internal ambassadors (pilot)
  • Teams/Slack notifications
Early Adopters
  • Sites with parking pressure
  • Teams with similar hours
  • Companies with sustainability targets
Cost Structure
  • Build & maintenance (MVP)
  • Infra/hosting
  • IT support & incident management
  • Internal comms & adoption
  • Evolution (matching, metrics, incentives)
Revenue Streams / Benefits
  • No external monetization (internal benefit)
  • Lower parking/commute cost
  • ESG/RSC compliance & brand
  • Employee wellbeing & retention
